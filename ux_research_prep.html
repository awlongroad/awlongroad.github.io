Application Research Task

Preparation for UX/UI Analysis

Time Required: 30 minutes total (10 minutes per application)

Purpose: To build detailed application profiles for tomorrow's UX/UI analysis lesson

What You'll Need This For

Tomorrow we'll be analysing user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design. Your research today will help you:

• Understand how different application types serve different user needs

• Identify design patterns and interface elements

• Evaluate what makes applications easy or difficult to use

• Connect theory to real applications you know well

Step 2: Research Questions for Your Analysis

For each of your 3 chosen categories, you'll need to answer these specific research questions. This will take approximately 10 minutes per application.

Research Tips

  • Choose applications you genuinely use - your experience is valuable data
  • Be specific in your observations rather than giving general statements
  • Think about recent updates or changes you've noticed
  • Consider both what works well and what frustrates you
  • Take screenshots if helpful to refer to during tomorrow's lesson

Application: WhatsApp

Key Observation: Simple interface with green colour scheme, large buttons for calls, and easy access to camera for quick photo sharing. The status feature competes with Instagram stories but feels integrated naturally.
